      <h2>Acquisition Modality Codes</h2>
      <div>
        <p>This Value Set includes codes that may be used to identify an image or waveform acquisition
           modality, as used in the ImagingStudy resource, Dicom  Attribute Modality (0008,0060)
           or HL7 v2 Table 0259 (see HL7 v2.6 Chapter 8 Section 8.8.8.47). It generally corresponds
           to a class of diagnostic equipment, or to a specific acquisition function or technique
           in a device.</p>
